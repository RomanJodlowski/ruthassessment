NEWHAVEN HARBOUR

Place you want to visit

Something you would like to know

This day's tourists who are visiting Edinburgh cannot forget about the most changed district from the time when Newhaven was a small separate village and nowadays is indistinguishable from rest of the city, however, there is much quieter than in neighbouring Leith.
Beneath the overview of modern development like Platinum Point or industrial part of the area, it is very possible to see the old signs of the previous life as Newhaven Harbour or the Main Street buildings.

Although Newhaven seems to be a more industrial part of the city there is an excellent place to spend time with your loved ones in the parks, restaurants, pubs even walk around the shore.
Due to the Edinburgh Council, this area is the most developing part of the city where a lot of new housing estates and new investments are built, such as a tram connection with the airport or a film studio.
